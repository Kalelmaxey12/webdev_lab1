About Me

Hello! My name is Kalel Maxey, and I’m a student-athlete at the Illinois Institute of Technology. I play basketball for my school, and it’s a huge part of my life. Ever since I was a kid, basketball has been my passion. I love the competition, the teamwork, and the focus it takes to get better every single day.

I’m 6'7" tall and known for being athletic and hardworking. My goal is to make it to the NBA one day. I know that it takes discipline, sacrifice, and determination to reach that level, but I’m committed to giving it my all. My experience as a college athlete has helped me build confidence, responsibility, and leadership skills that go beyond the court.

My Basketball Journey

I started playing basketball when I was young, and over time it became something that shaped my identity. At Illinois Tech, I’ve learned how to manage both academics and athletics. Balancing late-night practices, early-morning workouts, and classes can be tough, but it has taught me time management and mental strength.

Even when things get hard, I remind myself why I started playing: for the love of the game and the challenge of becoming the best version of myself. Basketball has introduced me to great teammates, mentors, and opportunities to grow as a person.
